How it began

We started this because
Asia deserves to tell its own stories.

In 2019, we noticed something. The most thoughtful, talented people across Asia were doing remarkable work. Quietly. Without the storytelling to share it, and often without the mentors to help them grow into it.

So we started Stories of Asia. A small platform with a simple belief: that this region's people deserve to find their voice through storytelling, and to grow into that voice through mentorship and community.

Seven years in, that belief still shapes everything we do.

Today we're a home for leadership development, technology, and a social mentoring community. But underneath it all, the work is the same: helping the people of Asia tell their stories, find their support, and build the kind of careers and businesses worth telling stories about.
